We need to ask you some questions to verify your identity

We’ll ask you some security questions that only you should know the answers to. These will be based on information you’ve already given us.

We will not keep your answers.

We'll do this to:

  • keep your personal details secure
  • protect your account against fraud
  • provide you with a faster service

So we can confirm your identity, you may need to have the following information available:

  • details of benefits you receive
  • your partner’s details
  • past or present employment information

Try to to answer these questions as accurately as you can. You will not be able to change your answers if you make a mistake

If you do not enter anything for more than 30 minutes, your application will time out and you’ll have to start again. You cannot save your application and come back later.
