We need you to answer some security questions

So we can confirm your identity, we’ll ask you some questions that only you should know the answers to. These will be based on information you’ve previously given to the Department of Work and Pensions.

So we can confirm your identity, you may need to have the following information available:

  • details of benefits you receive
  • your partner’s details
  • past or present employment information
! Warning Try to to answer these questions as accurately as you can. You will not be able to change your answers if you make a mistake

We will not keep your answers.

How we know what questions to ask you
We’ll work with Department of Work and Pensions (opens in a new tab) to make sure we ask you questions that only you can answer. This is because they have access to information that we can check your answers against.
Read our privacy notice (opens in a new tab) if you’d like to know more about how the details will be used to prove your identity.
