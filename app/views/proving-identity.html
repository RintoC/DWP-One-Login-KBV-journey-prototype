Proving your identity

You can set up a digital identity to show you’re who you say you are.

We use digital identities to:

  • keep your personal details secure
  • protect your account against fraud
  • provide you with a faster service

So we can confirm your identity, you may need to have the following information available

  • details of your Universal Credit claim. This can include dependants and partner details
  • past or present employment information

If you do not enter anything for more than 30 minutes, your application will time out and you’ll have to start again.

You cannot save your application and come back later.
